Hidden Lever

You grab the frame of the painting, realizing that it is hung up only over a nail. There is a lever in a gap behind the painting. Upon pulling the lever, you hear an intercom become active:

"Welcome to my fun house. I have locked you in and you must escape. Find all of the hidden envelopes around this house, and answer the questions out loud. If you get them right, I will say a number, take down this number with the pen and paper you will find in your back pocket, I gave you them while you were still out cold. You will see a door At the end of the hall, once you have collected all of the numbers, multiply them together, it will take you a while with no calculator! The product of this will reveal the 8 digit passcode required to unlock it. Get it wrong and you will be kept here even longer, and will have to try again to escape."

Choose your action.
