Welcome to Holiday Horror!

This is a text and image based escape room game. Your goal is to explore the areas within the house in order to find out how you can escape.

You wake up in a hallway with no recollection of how you got there. The last thing you remember is the excitement for you holiday before the lights on your bus begin flickering and everything went dark.

When you are ready 2 begin, please select the correct start button.
